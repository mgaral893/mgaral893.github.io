HandsOff: Anti-Theft Alarm

An anti-theft alarm for Android. Triggers the moment someone touches, unplugs, or steals your phone. Only your 4-digit PIN stops it. 100% on-device — no account, no backend, no data leaves your phone.

Android Security Privacy-aware No ads SDK

What it does

HandsOff uses physical sensors (accelerometer, proximity, light) to react the moment someone touches, unplugs, or steals your phone from your pocket.

  • Motion sensor — triggers on any movement.
  • Charger alarm — triggers if the cable is unplugged while armed.
  • Pocket mode — triggers if your phone is taken out of your pocket in the dark.
  • Headphone alert — triggers if someone removes your earbuds while armed.

Unsilenceable alarm

Software-generated 95 dB siren — no mp3 files. Three ringtones (Police, Nuclear, Alien). Strobe flash and continuous vibration until PIN is entered. Volume is locked at max during alarm.

Privacy

All app settings (mode, sensor preferences, PIN, armed state) are stored locally and removed when the app is uninstalled. The app does not require a user account and does not operate its own backend server for personal data.
